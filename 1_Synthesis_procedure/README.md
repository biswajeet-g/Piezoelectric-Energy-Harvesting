# Synthesis of PVDF-HFP-BCZT-Mn-Mxene Nanocomposite Films

## Materials required
| Materials | Description | Amount |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|
| PVDF-HFP | Piezoelectric Polymer Matrix (Flexible) | 1.6gm |
| BCZT | Lead Free ferroelectric ceramic with high d33 | 40% of PVDF i.e. 0.64gm |
| Mn (Manganese Dopant) | Improves ferroelectric properties, reduces leakage current, stabilizes crystal structure | - |
| Mxene | Conductive 2D filler | 0.5%, 1%, 1.5%, 2% of PVDF-HFP + BCZT-Mn Mixture |
| DMF | Solvent for dissolving PVDF | 20ml |


## Step Involved
- DMF was added of 20ml into a 50ml beaker.
- The required Mxene to the solvent at 80*C and 1000 RPM in the beaker, placed on magnetic stirrer.
- The Solution was sonicated for 15mins afer each 1hr to 1.5hrs.
- Stirring of Mxene would be taking place for almost 24 hrs.
- The PVDF tablets were added into the solution and left for dissolution for 1hr to 1.5hrs.
- The solution was sonicated and then BCZT-Mn ceramic was added. The solution would turn into pale yellow colour.
- Repeated stirring and sonicaton of the composite happened for more 24 hrs.
  - ##### NOTE : if the volume of the composite goes below 20ml, due to evaporation, more 5-10ml of DMF can be added.
- The solution was kept in the hot oven for 12 hrs at 80*C
- After heating in the oven, the composite was again stirred for an hour.
- Then the composite was spreaded over a glass sheet with a glass rod, then heated it in oven for 6 hrs.
- Finally, it produces a nanocomposite thin sheet.
