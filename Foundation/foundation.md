### 1. Electric Field
-> Electric Field (E) is the electrical force per unit charge applied across the thickness of the PVDF–BCZT composite film.
-> E= d/v
where:
E = Electric field (V/m or kV/cm)
V = Applied voltage
d = Thickness of the film
