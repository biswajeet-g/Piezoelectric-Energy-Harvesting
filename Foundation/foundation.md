## Foundation of the Project

### Topics to be covered:
- Electric Field
- Dipole
- Polarization
- Dielectric Materials
- Ferroelectricity
- Piezoelectricity

---

### 1. Electric Field
- Electric Field (E) is the electrical force per unit charge applied across the thickness of the PVDF–BCZT composite film.
- E = d/v
-- where:
  - E = Electric field (V/m or kV/cm)
  - V = Applied voltage
  - d = Thickness of the film

### 2. Dipole
- Positive and negative charges are separated by a small distance.
-- Dipole Moment: The strength of the dipole is measured by
  p = q x d
  - where,
    - q = charge
    - d = separation distance
